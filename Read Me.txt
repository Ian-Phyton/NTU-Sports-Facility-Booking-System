--------------------------- SETUP ---------------------------------
Make sure to have Python3 installed.
Run setup.bat file.
Change username and password in booking.py

--------------------------- HOW TO USE ----------------------------------
1. When booking.py is started, you will choose your preferred time, date and court.
2. It will book the following slot at exactly midnight 12:00am.
3. Keep the script running until midnight 12:00pm for the booking to happen.
